French fries are crispy, golden potato sticks fried until crunchy on the outside and soft on the inside. They are one of the most popular snacks in the world.

They are easy to make, quick to cook, and taste great with ketchup, mayo, or any dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 large potatoes
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin sticks.
  2. Wash the potato sticks in cold water to remove extra starch.
  3. Dry them with a clean towel.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan.
  5. Fry the potatoes in batches until light golden.
  6. Remove and let them rest for 2 minutes.
  7. Fry again until crispy and golden brown.
  8. Remove from oil and sprinkle with salt.
  9. Serve hot with your favorite sauce.
