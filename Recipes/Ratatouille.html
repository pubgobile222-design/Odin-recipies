Recipies Of Ratatouille


Description

Ratatouille Ratatouille is a traditional French vegetable dish made with sliced zucchini, eggplant, tomatoes, and bell peppers baked in a rich tomato sauce. It is colorful, healthy, and full of natural flavor.

This dish is simple, comforting, and perfect as a main meal or side dish. The vegetables soften as they bake, creating a warm and delicious meal that tastes even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 eggplant
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon dried herbs (thyme or basil)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the zucchini, eggplant, tomatoes, and bell pepper.
  3. Chop the onion and garlic.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  5. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and herbs. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Spread the sauce in a baking dish.
  7. Arrange the sliced vegetables on top of the sauce.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake for 10 more minutes.
  10. Let it cool slightly, then serve.
